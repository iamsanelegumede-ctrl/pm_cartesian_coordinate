1.4 - A circle has a center that lies at the origin and its radius is 4. Provide the coordinates of the points where the circle intersects the x-axis. (2 marks)
x2+y2=r2
x2+y2=16
y=0
So: x2=16 ⟹ x=±4 (4,0) and (−4,0)
